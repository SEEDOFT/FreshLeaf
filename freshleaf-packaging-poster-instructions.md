# FreshLeaf Organics Packaging Poster Instructions

## Goal

Create a modern, easy-to-understand, and attractive packaging poster for **FreshLeaf Organics**. The poster should present FreshLeaf as a trusted Cambodian organic vegetable marketplace that connects customers with verified local vendors through a smart mobile app.

The design should feel fresh, clean, practical, and premium enough for a technology-enabled food marketplace. It should work as a promotional poster for packaging, delivery bags, product inserts, social media, or app marketing materials.

## Core Message

FreshLeaf helps customers buy fresh organic vegetables and herbs from trusted local vendors with a simple mobile shopping experience.

The poster should communicate these ideas quickly:

- Fresh organic vegetables and herbs
- Verified local vendors
- Easy mobile ordering
- Wallet and local payment support
- Order tracking from preparation to delivery
- AI-assisted shopping help
- Customer support chat
- Friendly experience for English and Khmer users

## Poster Copy

Use this main headline:

**FreshLeaf Organics**

Use this tagline:

**Fresh vegetables from trusted local vendors, delivered through one smart app.**

Use this short supporting message:

Shop organic vegetables, manage your cart, pay easily, track your order, and get help from FreshLeaf support whenever you need it.

Use this call-to-action:

**Shop fresh. Eat better. Support local growers.**

Optional short labels for feature icons:

- Fresh Produce
- Verified Vendors
- Easy Ordering
- Wallet Payment
- Order Tracking
- AI Shopping Help
- Support Chat
- EN / KM Friendly

## Visual Direction

Use a clean natural design with a modern app-marketplace feeling.

Recommended style:

- Bright green and white as the main palette
- Small warm accents such as yellow, lime, or light orange
- Fresh vegetable photography or realistic produce illustration
- A mobile app mockup showing browsing, cart, or checkout
- Soft shadows, clean spacing, and rounded modern UI elements
- Simple icons for features instead of long text blocks
- A trustworthy and friendly tone, not a luxury-only or supermarket-only look

Recommended visual assets:

- Basket or arrangement of leafy greens, cucumber, tomato, carrot, cabbage, herbs, and other Cambodian market vegetables
- A phone screen showing product browsing or checkout
- Small vendor/trust badge element
- Wallet/payment icon
- Chat or AI assistant icon

## Feature Highlights

The poster should highlight FreshLeaf as more than a simple vegetable shop:

- **Marketplace:** Customers buy from approved vendors through one platform.
- **Fresh Produce:** Focus on organic vegetables, herbs, and everyday healthy ingredients.
- **Mobile App:** Customers can browse products, manage cart items, checkout, and track orders.
- **Payments:** Support wallet and local payment workflows.
- **Trust:** Vendors are verified before selling on the platform.
- **Assistance:** AI shopping help and customer support chat make the experience easier.
- **Cambodia Focus:** The product is designed for Cambodian customers and can support English and Khmer communication.

## Layout Guidance

Use a clear poster structure:

1. Place the FreshLeaf Organics name at the top as the strongest brand element.
2. Use fresh produce as the hero image so the product category is instantly clear.
3. Add a mobile app mockup beside or below the produce image.
4. Place the tagline close to the headline.
5. Use 4 to 8 compact feature icons in a neat row or grid.
6. Put the call-to-action near the bottom.
7. Leave enough white space so the design stays clean and readable.

The poster should be readable from a distance. Keep text short, use strong contrast, and avoid crowding the layout.

## Do Not Include

Do not include claims that are not confirmed by project data:

- Exact product prices
- Exact discount percentages
- Exact delivery times
- Specific vendor names
- Current stock availability
- Wallet balances
- Live order statuses
- Certification claims such as "certified organic" unless real certification data is provided

Avoid these design problems:

- Generic supermarket poster style
- Too many paragraphs
- Dark, heavy, or muddy colors
- Overly complex tech diagrams
- Fake app screens that promise unsupported features
- Visuals that make the product look like imported luxury groceries instead of a practical Cambodian fresh-produce marketplace

## Final Prompt

Create a modern packaging poster for **FreshLeaf Organics**, a Cambodian B2C organic vegetable marketplace mobile app.

The poster should show fresh organic vegetables and herbs, trusted verified local vendors, and a clean mobile app shopping experience. Include a strong hero image of fresh produce, a phone mockup showing product browsing or checkout, and simple feature icons for fresh produce, verified vendors, easy ordering, wallet payment, order tracking, AI shopping help, support chat, and English/Khmer friendliness.

Use the headline **FreshLeaf Organics** and the tagline **Fresh vegetables from trusted local vendors, delivered through one smart app.** Add the call-to-action **Shop fresh. Eat better. Support local growers.**

Use a bright green and white color palette with small warm accents. The design should feel clean, natural, trustworthy, modern, and easy to understand. Make it attractive for packaging, delivery bags, product inserts, or social media promotion.

Do not invent exact prices, discounts, delivery times, vendor names, stock availability, wallet balances, live order statuses, or organic certification claims unless verified project data is provided.
